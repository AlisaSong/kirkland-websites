OUR PROCESS


>>

Consultation

Upon receiving your inquiry, we will reach out to you within 48 hours to set up a 30-minute consultation. Together, we will figure out a tailored recommendation of work that fits your business needs.

Timeline and Agreement

Once we decide on the work ahead, we will set up a quick timeline of deliverables and get started! Typically we can build a small business website over a 1-2 month period, but this timing may vary depending on your situation. For photography-heavy projects, we will set up specific times for photoshoots that work best with your schedule.

Collaboration

Once our collaboration starts, we will check in with you to review our work over the course of the project. You'll have the opportunity to provide feedback as we build out your site and/or edit your visuals.

Final Delivery

We'll deliver the website you've been waiting for, making sure that web hosting, domain name service, and the site itself is up and ready to go.
